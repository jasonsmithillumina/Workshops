BaseSpace Tutorial : RNAExpress App: Hands-On Exercise

This tutorial will provide a walk-through the RNAExpress App in BaseSpace

 

Objectives:

 

  • Review Inputs for RNAExpress BaseSpace App
  • Inspect results from RNAExpress BaseSpace App

    •  

     

    Conventions used:
    Brackets <html element>
    Bold Text BaseSpace special terms
    Quotes“Literal On-Screen Text”
    U/I/BUser Action

    Step 1: Access BaseSpace  

    Open a Web Browser to http://goo.gl/qDHM7o

     

    ***If Needed:

  • Create BaseSpace account or Login
  • Accept Project
    (This contains completed RNA Express Analyses and Data)

    •  

    Step 2: Launch Inputs for RNA Express App

     

  • Click on “Launch App” RocketShip button

    •  

  • Select on “RNA Express App”

    •  

    Step 3: Specify Control Samples for RNA Express

     

  • Scroll to “Control Group” section

    •  

  • Click to “Select Samples(s)” <drop-down> menu

    •  

    Step 4: Select Samples for Analysis / Utilize Filter

     

  • Click on “Project” <drop-down> menu to filter displayed samples by Project name

    •  

  • Select Both “UHR” samples as input for Control Samples

    •  

  • Click on “Confirm” button

    		•

    Step 5: Specify Comparison (condition) Samples for Analysis

     

  • Select Both “Brain” samples as input for Comparison Samples

    •  

    Step 6: Navigate to completed RNA Express Analysis

     

    You will now be viewing a pre-computed RNAExpress Analysis from the project you accepted in Step 1

     

  • Navigate to “..RNA-Seq” Project within BaseSpace

    •  

    Step 7: Open RNAExpress Analysis

     

  • Click RNA Express analysis :

     “RNA Express Brain v UHRR”

    •  

    Step 8: Orient within RNA Express Analysis

     

  • Click “Analysis info”

  • Question 1: How long did this analysis take? Record your answer.

     

    		•

    Step 9: Explore Summary Report within RNA Express Analysis

     

  • Click “Summary” under “Analysis Reports”

  • Answer the following questions, Record your Answers

    •  

  • Question 2: What is the Differentially Expressed Gene Count?

     

  • Question 3: What percentage of reads mapped to splice sites in “UHR-Access-1”?

     

  • Question 4: What is the defintion of “q-value” in the Control/Comparison plot?

    (Hint: Mouse-over the BLUE i ICONS to view ToolTips and Definitions within the report

     

    		•

    Step 10: Interact with Comparison Plot

     

  • Slide <blue selector bar>.
    Notice how the plot changes dynamically.

     

  • Click any blue dot on the plot.
    Notice how the table below highlights your selection.

     

  • Type “PARK7” in to <text fieldr>.
    Notice single row in the table and focus plot

     

  • Question 5: What is the log2(Fold Change) of PARK7?

     

    		•

    Step 11 (optional): Review raw output files from RNA Express

     

     

  • Click “Output Files”

     

  • Navigate to “RNAExpress-AppResults > differential > UHRR vs Brain”

     

  • Examine/Download Raw Data in .PDF/.CSV/ETC.

     

    Note**: Simple Excel files shown in screenshot can be created from raw data.

     

     

    Congratulations. This concludes the RNAExpress Tutorial!
